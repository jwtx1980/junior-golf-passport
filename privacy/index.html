Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Junior Golf Passport is being built as a parent-managed profile and memory platform for junior golfers. This early website is a static preview and does not currently collect account information directly.

Information We Expect To Collect In The Product

Future versions may collect parent account details, golfer profile information, course logs, scores, achievements, tournament results, notes, photos, and privacy preferences.

How Information Will Be Used

Information will be used to create and manage golfer passport pages, display course and achievement history, support sharing controls, and provide customer support.

Junior Golfer Privacy

Because this product involves minors, profiles should be managed by a parent or guardian. The product should support public, private, and unlisted profile settings before accepting real user accounts.

Contact

Questions can be sent through the support page while the product is in early development.
