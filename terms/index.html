Terms of Use

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Junior Golf Passport is an early-stage website and product concept for tracking junior golfer courses, milestones, rounds, and memories.

Parent-Managed Use

Profiles for junior golfers should be created and managed by a parent or guardian. Users are responsible for choosing what information is appropriate to publish or share.

Content

Users should only upload or publish content they have the right to use, including photos, notes, scores, and tournament information.

No Recruiting Or Performance Guarantee

Junior Golf Passport can help organize a golfer's journey, but it does not guarantee tournament results, coach interest, school placement, or recruiting outcomes.

Changes

These terms will be updated as the product moves from static preview to a working application with accounts and stored data.
