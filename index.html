What it is

A golf resume, scrapbook, course map, and achievement tracker in one place.

The first version focuses on beautiful player pages that parents can maintain without turning golf memories into spreadsheet work. It is personal enough for family and polished enough to share with coaches, schools, teammates, and friends.

Player Profile

Name, photo, region, graduation year, team, home course, goals, and share controls.

Course Passport

Played states, course pins, dates, course notes, photos, and favorite memories.

Milestones

First par, first birdie, low scores, tournament wins, and custom family achievements.

Round Timeline

Memorable rounds, scorecards, trophies, golf trips, best shots, and tournament days.
